Schuylkill Waterpark

The first in a series of imaginary structures, this set of waterslides and swim lanes poised confidently over Philadelphia's Western river seeks to provoke questions about the Schuylkill's ecological and accessibility conditions. Absurd only in contrast to the river's existing state, the Schuylkill Water Park encourages us to unpack the gap between reality and its enticing fiction.
